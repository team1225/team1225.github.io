Setup ClickUp

Use the information on this page to set up your ClickUp Account
Notice

The steps below require a Google Workspace account. Setup Google Workspace before continuing
Go to Google Workspace Setup

  1. Click the link below to open the ClickUp signup page
    ClickUp SSO Signup

  2. Clck the purple signup with Google button. Then confirm your Google Account.

  3. Next choose a password. Your password must be 8 characters long and must contain at least 1 capital letter.
    (Note: Even though you are setting a password you can only use Google SSO to login)

  4. Check your Team 1225 email with the link below and enter the verification code that ClickUp has sent you.
    Team 1225 Email

  5. After you enter the verifcation code ClickUp will then ask you to join Team 1225. Click "Yes, join Team 1225, The Gorillas"

    6. Onboarding Instructions

  6. You have now joined ClickUp!
    Click the Green button to continue

  7. Choose your profile picture, then click "I'm happy so far"

  8. Next you will choose the color theme for your account. If you don't want to pick a theme click "I'm happy so far" and the default 1225 Green will be applied.

  9. Since this ClickUp account has already been setup just click "No Thanks"

  10. That's it! Click the "Play with ClickUp button" and ClickUp will apply your settings.
