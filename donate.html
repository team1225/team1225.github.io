Ways to Donate

For your convenience, we have many ways you can donate to our team. If you require assistance in the donation process, please contact us at funds@team1225.com and we will gladly assist you.


  1. Donate through PayPal

  2. Boys and Girls CLub of Henderson County
    To make any size tax-deductible contribution:
    1. Make Check to Boys and Girls Club of Henderson County
    2. Put "Robotics" in the Memo Line
    3. Mail Check to: Boys & Girls Club of Henderson County. PO Box 1460 Hendersonville, NC 28793

  3. FIRST North Carolina
    To make a sizable tax-deductible contribution for sponsorship:
    1. Use the link HERE
    2. Click the Grey donate box
    3. Fill out the form and designate "Team 1225, The Gorillas" in the comments section.

If you are in need of a 501(c)(3) number, please contact us at funds@team1225.com and we will assist you with the donation.
