Sponsor Us

Sponsorship is the backbone of our team. Whether it is monetary, tangible, or intangible; all contributions are massively appreciated. Our team relies on the kindness of sponsors, who make it possible for us to continue innovating the way we are, and inspiring future leaders in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math.

If you are looking to sponsor our team please fill out our interest form below. If you need a document to show your company or place of business please take a look at our sponsorship package by clicking HERE to open the PDF.
